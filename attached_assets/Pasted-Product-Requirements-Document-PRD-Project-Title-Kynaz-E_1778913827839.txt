Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Project Title

Kynaz Enterprise Integrated Service & Cashback Portal

1. Project Overview
Purpose

The purpose of this project is to develop a centralized web platform for Kynaz Enterprise that allows customers to:

Request quotations
Purchase and renew services
Track submissions
Receive cashback rewards
Manage policies and renewals
Communicate with admin via website, WhatsApp, and email

The system will also provide an advanced admin portal for managing customers, quotations, services, cashback, and notifications.

2. Business Objectives
Primary Objectives
Digitize customer service process
Increase customer retention using cashback rewards
Automate quotation workflow
Improve customer trust and convenience
Centralize document and submission management
Increase recurring renewals
Reduce manual admin work
